Refund Policy

Effective Date: 9 August 2025

At Swift Fortress (“we”, “our”, “us”), we are committed to providing high-quality services and products. This Refund Policy outlines your rights and our obligations under UK consumer law and our payment processors’ terms (including Stripe).

1. Eligibility for Refunds

  • You are entitled to request a refund within 14 days of purchase for digital services, provided the service has not been fully delivered or accessed.
  • For physical goods, returns are accepted within 14 days of receipt, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging.
  • Custom or bespoke work is non-refundable once production has commenced, unless required by law.

2. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, contact us at swiftfortress@proton.me with:

  • Your full name
  • Order number
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund request

3. Processing Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed via the original payment method within 7–10 business days. Stripe’s processing times may vary depending on your bank or card provider.

4. Exceptions

  • Refunds will not be issued for change of mind after services have been delivered in full.
  • Any misuse, damage, or alteration of goods will void eligibility for refunds.
